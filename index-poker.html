CARD · 004 · KING OF SPADES · EXPERIENCE

The casino story, formalized.

You asked about the Kelly fraction. Here is the real equation that got me hired.

The casino was running an ostensibly-fair shuffle. Three hundred hands in, I noticed the deal sequences had a periodicity — the PRNG seed space was smaller than the deck's entropy. A pattern existed. I modeled it:

P ( exploit  |  patternn )  =  1 − ∏i=1n ( 1 − pi )

After 50 observations, predicting the next deck was 84% accurate. With p = 0.52 per hand and 1:1 payout, Kelly said to bet 4% of bankroll per play. I never took the money. I wrote it up, sent it to the casino's CTO, and they hired me three months later to patch it quietly before it shipped to mainnet.

Timeline. Summer '22: exploit. Fall '22: NDA, patch. '23 onward: I've been quiet about which casino it was, but I still have the Kelly spreadsheet.
